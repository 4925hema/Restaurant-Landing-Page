Our Specials

Alva Puri

Alva Puri is a traditional festival combination that brings together crispy golden puris and sweet, melt-in-the-mouth halwa. This comforting dish is a popular offering during religious functions and special occasions in South India.

Kaja

Kaja is a beloved sweet treat from Andhra Pradesh, made with crispy, flaky layers of dough deep-fried to perfection and then dipped in fragrant sugar syrup. The result is a melt-in-the-mouth experience that’s both crunchy and juicy.

Pootha Rakulu

Potha Rakulu is a traditional Andhra fish delicacy made with fresh Murrel (Potha) fish, simmered in a fiery tamarind and spice gravy. This rustic and flavorful dish is a favorite among seafood lovers and is typically enjoyed with hot steamed rice or ragi sangati.

About Us

Many chefs blend ancient techniques with modern presentations, showcasing India’s spices, herbs, and cooking heritage globally. Renowned Indian chefs like Sanjeev Kapoor, Vikas Khanna, Ranveer Brar, and Garima Arora have brought Indian food to international fame, promoting local ingredients and traditional methods while also exploring global fusion.

From rich Mughlai dishes in the north to the spicy curries of the south, and from coastal seafood delights in the west to steamed delicacies in the east, Indian chefs master a wide range of regional cuisines..
